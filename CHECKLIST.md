# Factorio Space Age — World-by-World Speedrun Checklists

A terse, execution-oriented checklist set for fast progression.

Principles:

* Always be building.
* Expand before starvation.
* Overproduce power early.
* Hand-craft only to bootstrap automation.
* Build for throughput, not aesthetics.
* Bots replace spaghetti.
* Buffer critical intermediates.
* Never let research idle.

---

## Goal

Bootstrap a scalable industrial base, launch first rockets, establish orbital logistics, and prepare exports for other planets.

Target:

* Casual fast run: 6-10h
* Strong run: 3-6h
* Top-tier Space Age split: <1h to first departure

# GLOBAL_SPEEDRUN_RULES.md

## Universal Priorities

1. Power
2. Mining
3. Smelting
4. Circuits
5. Science
6. Logistics
7. Rocket throughput

---

## Throughput Heuristics

### Iron

* Always insufficient
* Double production before needed

### Copper

* Needed earlier than expected

### Steel

* Start early
* Scale continuously

### Circuits

* Green circuits become infrastructure
* Red circuits bottleneck expansion
* Blue circuits bottleneck victory

---

## Combat Philosophy

* Minimal combat early
* Clear only necessary nests
* Use efficiency over brute force
* Tank timing is huge
